American National Emissions By Source Category

Generally, emissions of air pollution come from: stationary fuel combustion sources (like: electric utilities, industrial boilers), industrial and other processes (like: metal smelters, petroleum refineries, cement kilns and dry cleaners), highway vehicles, non-road mobile sources (like: marine vessels, aircraft and locomotives)

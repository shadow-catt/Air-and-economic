Economic Strength with Cleaner Air

Between 1970 and 2020, the combined emissions of the six common pollutants (PM2.5 and PM10, SO2, NOx, VOCs, CO and Pb) dropped by 78 percent. This progress occurred while U.S. economic indicators remain strong. And the following are the output for each indicators


Index: 2017
● Gross Domestic Product: 2.664541485%
● Vehicle Miles Traveled: 1.894006306%
● Population: 0.590243902%
● Energy Consumption: 0.439150132%
● CO2 Emissions: 0.203269704%
● Aggregate Emissions (Six Common Pollutants): -0.75355266%
